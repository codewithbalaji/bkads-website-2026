## Platform

web

## Users

Primary visitor: the business owner/founder at a small or medium business,
manufacturing company, or a business undergoing digital transformation —
landing directly on the site to evaluate BKADS themselves (not an
intermediary evaluator). Based in or around Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India.

Their situation: stuck with manual/repetitive processes, outdated software,
poor data visibility/reporting, and difficulty adopting AI — looking for a
practical technology partner rather than a large, impersonal vendor.

## Product Purpose

BKADS is a Chennai-based technology services company. This site is its
marketing landing page — the job it does is convert visiting SMB/manufacturing
owners into leads (calls booked, contact made) for BKADS's services: AI
automation, website design & development, app & software development, SEO &
digital marketing, and e-commerce development. Success = inquiries/bookings
from Year 1 target customers, plus establishing brand presence.

## Positioning

BKADS combines modern software development, AI expertise, and business-focused
problem solving under one roof — the practical, honest, long-term-support
alternative to hiring multiple disconnected vendors (a freelance web dev, a
separate AI consultant, a separate marketing agency) or an impersonal large
agency. A neighboring product could not truthfully claim the same breadth
(automation + apps + software + SEO + e-commerce) delivered with the same
"we treat your business challenge as our own" personal commitment.

## Product Principles

1. Breadth with coherence — one partner covers automation, web, apps,
   software, marketing, and e-commerce, not a fragmented vendor list.
2. Practical over flashy — solutions solve a stated operational problem
   (manual work, poor visibility, outdated systems), not technology for its
   own sake.
3. Earned trust over inflated claims — an early-stage company should read as
   honest and credible, not as a large, long-established agency.
4. Personal accountability — "your business challenge is our challenge";
   long-term support, not a one-off handoff.
